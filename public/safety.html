Safety Guidelines

Last updated: December 28, 2025

1. Be respectful

Treat others with respect. Harassment, threats, targeted abuse, or behavior intended to shame or intimidate people is not allowed.

2. No illegal or harmful content

Do not use Whisp to share content that is illegal, encourages illegal activity, or puts others at risk. This includes fraud/scams, sharing private personal information (doxxing), or instructions for wrongdoing.

3. No hate or discrimination

Content that promotes hatred or discrimination against protected groups is not allowed.

4. No spam or manipulation

Don’t spam, mislead, or artificially boost content. Avoid repetitive posts, unsolicited promotions, or attempts to game visibility.

5. Respect privacy

Don’t share anyone’s private information. If someone asks you to stop contacting them, you should stop.

6. Reporting and enforcement

If you see content that violates these guidelines, report it using the in-app tools (when available). We may remove content, limit features, or suspend accounts that violate our rules or applicable law.

7. You are responsible

You are responsible for what you post and how you use the platform. Using Whisp is a privilege—help keep local spaces safe and positive.
