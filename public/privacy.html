Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Whisp (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our services (the “Service”). We process personal data according to applicable law and we are GDPR compliant.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:

  • Account information (e.g., identifiers needed to create and manage your account).
  • Content you provide (posts, messages, reports, and other content you submit).
  • Usage and device data (basic analytics, logs, diagnostics, and security events).
  • Location-related data where necessary to provide location-based features (as applicable).

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Secure the Service, prevent fraud/abuse, and enforce our policies.
  • Respond to requests and support inquiries.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we process personal data under one or more legal bases such as: performance of a contract (providing the Service), legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention), consent (where required), and legal obligation.

5. Sharing of information

We may share information with service providers who process data on our behalf (for example, hosting and security) and when required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When you delete your account, we delete the personal information associated with your account from our active systems. Limited information may be retained where required/permitted by law (e.g., security and compliance).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to processing of your personal data, and to request data portability. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the official support channel provided within the Service.
